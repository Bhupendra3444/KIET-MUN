Message from Secretary General

Vaibhav Chaudhary
Secretary General
KIET MUN'24

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors, Bringing the world's citizens together in a venue where disputes may be settled through involvement, communication, and understanding is urgently needed. Through MUNs, students learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. It involves and enhances research, public speaking, and debating skills, along with critical thinking, leadership abilities, and effective conflict resolution. We've always welcomed opportunities that would broaden the perspectives of our young, dynamic leaders beyond rote memorization and encourage them to explore the as-yet uncharted waters of international politics and policymaking. With this thought in mind, we welcome you to KIET MUN 2024 and its engaging and stimulating sessions ahead. Remember, as you embark on this journey: "DEBATE.DIPLOMACY.DISCUSSION". I wish you all the very best.

Sincerely,
Vaibhav Chaudhary
Secretary General
KIET Model united Nations 2024
