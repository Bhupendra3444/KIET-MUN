KIET Model United Nations Club

We proudly stand as the KIET University MUN Club, a dedicated cohort of students passionate about diplomacy and global affairs. With honor, we represent the esteemed KIET Model United Nations (KIETMUN) conference, now in its 6th edition in 2024, guided by the resounding motto of 'SYNERGY: Debate, Diplomacy, Discussion.' At KIETMUN, we strive for excellence through rigorous training and unwavering commitment to foster insightful dialogues and expand our horizons. Our journey is defined by the pursuit of embodying the core principles of the United Nations, fostering a culture of inclusivity and collaboration. Led by seasoned MUNers, we bring a wealth of practical experience to the table, poised to embrace every challenge and seize every opportunity for growth. As we embark on this MUN journey, our vision is clear: to cultivate a community of aspiring young diplomats, united in our dedication to shaping a brighter future through dialogue and cooperation. Join us as we champion the spirit of diplomacy and forge meaningful connections at KIETMUN 2024. Together, let us harness the power of synergy to inspire change and make our voices heard on the global stage.

Message from the Director General

Navya Yadav
Director General
KIETMUN Club

Welcome to the KIET Model United Nations Club! Embracing our motto of "synergy through debate, diplomacy, and discussion," the KIET MUN Club is an integral part of our university's commitment to holistic development. Drawing inspiration from KIET's ethos, the KIET MUN Club serves as a platform for nurturing leaders of the highest caliber. Over the years, Model United Nations (MUNs) have evolved into mainstream extracurricular activities, owing to their rigorous nature and the opportunity they provide for engaging in substantive debates. The KIET MUN Club takes proactive measures to promote the culture of MUNing within our university community. Through a variety of initiatives including Training Sessions and Intra-MUN events, we actively engage students and empower them with the skills necessary to excel in MUN conferences. With a strong and ever-growing membership base, the KIET MUN Club continues to make significant strides since its inception. Our success is not merely a reflection of the widespread acceptance of MUNs within our university, but also a testament to our commitment to enriching student minds with the values of critical thinking, diplomacy, and leadership. As members of the KIET MUN Club, we remain dedicated to our mission of fostering synergy through debate, diplomacy, and discussion. Together, let us continue to strive for excellence and uphold the ideals that define our club and our university community.

Warm Regards,
Navya Yadav
